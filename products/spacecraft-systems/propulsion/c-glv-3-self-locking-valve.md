## Overview

This C-GLV-3 self-locking valve provides an advanced solution for commercial spacecraft propulsion systems, designed to deliver dependable and economical propellant flow management with excellent space environmental compatibility. Based on the established GLV-3 low-friction self-locking design concept, this valve achieves outstanding performance in a streamlined configuration that optimizes mass and volume limitations for contemporary spacecraft operations. Strategic positioning between propellant tanks and thrusters, or as upstream control elements, enables accurate isolation and flow regulation essential for mission accomplishment.

The propulsion component integrates advanced low-friction torque mechanisms and axial flow engineering to achieve exceptional operational efficiency while preserving mechanical reliability throughout the 8-year service life. Self-locking functionality maintains secure valve positioning without constant power draw, substantially decreasing spacecraft power requirements while improving system safety. Developed specifically for commercial satellite applications, the C-GLV-3 valve demonstrates superior adaptation to demanding space conditions while sustaining consistent performance during extended missions.
